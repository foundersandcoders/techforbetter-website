How we work

IDEATE

We work with you to understand your product and identify the key features you need to create a successful MVP.

BUILD

We practice agile development, working in 'sprints' of two-week periods. This enables you to get new features out in front of your users quickly, gather feedback and then use this information to build a stronger product.

Handover

We meet with you at the end of every sprint to walk through the features we have built, collect your feedback and make sure that the product is ready for user testing.

ITERATE

We know that the early stages of building a product is full of learning. These underpin the next set of priority-1 features which will have the biggest impact on your product development and we use this to plan our next sprint.

Who are we?

We are a collective of developers, designers and product managers, all graduates of Founders & Coders. The Founders & Coders ethos of learning & improving is at the heart of everything we do. Give us a shout to find out more!
